Instant Pot Collard Greens

Description

Quick Southern-style collard greens made in your Instant Pot.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 pound smoked ham hocks
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pinch red pepper flakes
  • 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper, or to taste
  • 1 pinch paprika

Steps

  1. Turn on a multi-functional pressure cooker (such as Instant PotÂ®) and select Saute function.
  2. Add olive oil and heat until shimmering.
  3. Add ham hocks and sear on all sides, about 3 minutes per side.
  4. Add onion and garlic; cook and stir until onion is translucent, about 3 minutes.
  5. Add collard greens, red pepper flakes, salt, black pepper, cayenne pepper, and paprika.
  6. Close and lock the lid. Select high pressure according to manufacturer's instructions; set timer for 15 minutes. Allow 10 to 15 minutes for pressure to build.
  7. Release pressure using the natural-release method according to manufacturer's instructions, for 10 minutes. Release remaining pressure carefully using the quick-release method according to manufacturer's instructions, about 5 minutes. Unlock and remove the lid.
  8. Remove ham hocks from the pot and remove meat from the bones. Return meat to the pot and stir. Serve greens with a slotted spoon.

Black-eyed Peas Soup

Description

This black-eyed pea soup is a great New Year's meal and perfect on a cold winter night. Serve this comfort food with corn bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound dried black-eyed peas
  • 1/4 pound salt pork, diced
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 large clove garlic, minced
  • 1 bay leaf

Steps

  1. Wash peas and soak overnight in cold water.
  2. Drain peas and place in a large pot with 2 quarts of water.
  3. Add salt pork, onion, garlic, and bay leaf.
  4. Cover and simmer 2 hours, or until peas are tender.
  5. Remove bay leaf.
  6. Season to taste with salt and pepper.

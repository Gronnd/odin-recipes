Air Fryer Meatballs

Description

After trying air fryer meatballs, I will never bake them in the oven again. The insides stay nice and tender while the outsides get a slight crisp on them. The best part is that you can make them ahead of time and freeze for later use. I have included those directions in the notes. Dip meatballs in marinara, if desired.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup Italian seasoned bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped parsley
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) according to manufacturer's instructions.
  2. Combine ground beef, bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, parsley, egg, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Mix well and form into 1 1/2-inch meatballs.
  3. Place meatballs in a single layer in the basket of the air fryer. Cook in batches if necessary.
  4. Cook for 8 minutes. Flip and cook for 2 minutes more. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
